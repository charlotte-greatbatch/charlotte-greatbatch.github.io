Emotional Intelligence

What is emotional intelligence?

Emotional intelligence refers to the ability to perceive control and evaluate emotions within yourself and others. The 5 main components referenced in the book "Emotional Intelligence: Why it can matter more than IQ" written by Daniel Goleman are self awareness, self-regulation, motivation, empathy and social skills.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ can tell you whether you are working at an average, higher or lower mental capacity compared to your group/class/colleagues. EQ gives you a better understanding at how in-tune with yourself you are, whether you understand your own limits and capabillities emotionally.

Why is EQ important?

EQ is important in many ways, such as giving yourself the awareness of your current EQ, gives you the building blocks for growth in both your personal and professional life. It also gives you the awareness when things in life start to get tough so you can take a step back and breathe instead of getting lost in the stress and realising when it's too late.
